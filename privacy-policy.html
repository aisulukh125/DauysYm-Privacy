Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use DauysYm, our mobile application that translates Kazakh Sign Language (KSL) gestures into spoken language.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number (if provided via Clerk authentication).
  • Media Access: Camera, microphone, and gallery access to allow video recording and gesture recognition.
  • Uploaded Content: Videos recorded and uploaded for gesture translation.
  • Device Information: Device type, OS, and unique identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the DauysYm app experience.
  • To authenticate users securely using Clerk.
  • To process gesture recognition via our Flask backend.
  • To store and manage user data on Supabase.
  • To provide support and communicate updates.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers like Clerk and Supabase that help operate the app.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers and retained only as long as needed for the purposes described.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Submit a complaint to your local data protection authority.

6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be communicated through the app or email.

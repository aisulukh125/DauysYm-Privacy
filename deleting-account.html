Account Deletion

This page explains how users of the DauysYm app, developed by Dauysym Developers, can request deletion of their account and what happens to their data as a result.

1. How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the DauysYm app on your device.
  2. Navigate to Settings (gear icon in the top right corner).
  3. Select Account Management.
  4. Tap on Delete Account.
  5. Follow the on-screen steps to confirm deletion.

If you're unable to access the app, you can also request account deletion by email:

Email: aisulu.khairullayeva@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request

2. What Data Is Deleted

  • Deleted: Name, email, phone number, usage history, and personal preferences.
  • Retained: Anonymous, non-identifiable data (e.g., aggregated statistics), used only to improve services.

3. Data Deletion Timeline

Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 business days after we confirm your request. Some data may be stored longer if legally required.
